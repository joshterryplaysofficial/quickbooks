Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

App: Galaxy Nav App

1. Overview

Galaxy Nav App ("the App") is a personal-use tool that integrates with QuickBooks Online. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

2. Data We Access

The App accesses QuickBooks Online data through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 API, including:

  • Company financial information (invoices, expenses, payments)
  • Customer records
  • Account balances and reports

3. How Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to display financial information and perform actions you request. Data is processed locally on your own machine and is not transmitted to any third-party servers.

4. Data Storage

The App stores OAuth tokens locally in an encrypted database on your machine to maintain your authenticated session. No QuickBooks financial data is permanently stored by the App.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your data stays between your machine and Intuit's QuickBooks Online API.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as needed to maintain your connection. You can delete all stored tokens at any time by removing the local database file.

7. Security

The App uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 for authentication. Tokens are stored locally and are never exposed to external services.

8. Your Rights

You may disconnect the App from your QuickBooks Online account at any time through your Intuit account settings. This revokes all access immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app developer via the GitHub repository.
